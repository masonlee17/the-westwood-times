Legal Notice

This notice governs the editorial independence, content standards, and legal standing of The Westwood Times as an independent digital news organization.

The Westwood Times is an editorially independent publication. Our reporting and opinion content are produced without direction from advertisers, sponsors, or outside commercial interests. All content is subject to our editorial standards and fact-checking guidelines.

About The Westwood Times

The Westwood Times (TWT) is an independent digital news organization covering politics, world affairs, business, science, health, culture, and technology. Founded with a commitment to rigorous journalism and editorial integrity, TWT operates independently of any corporate parent, political party, or institutional interest.

All reporting published by The Westwood Times is the product of original newsgathering, editorial judgment, and journalistic standards consistent with accepted practices in the field. We are committed to accuracy, fairness, and transparency in our coverage.

Editorial Independence

The editorial operations of The Westwood Times are entirely separate from its business and advertising functions. Advertisers have no influence over editorial decisions, story selection, or the framing of news content. Our editors and reporters exercise independent judgment in all matters of coverage.

Opinion and commentary pieces published under named bylines represent the views of those individual contributors and do not necessarily reflect the institutional positions of The Westwood Times. Editorials published without a byline represent the collective view of our editorial board.

Accuracy and Corrections

The Westwood Times strives for accuracy in all published content. When errors are identified, we are committed to issuing timely corrections and, where appropriate, updating or annotating affected articles. Corrections are published prominently on the relevant article pages.

  • Factual errors will be corrected as soon as they are verified.
  • Significant corrections will be noted at the top of the affected article.
  • Minor typographical corrections may be made silently.
  • A correction log is maintained by the editorial team for internal accountability.

To report a factual error or request a correction, contact our editorial team at thewestwoodtimes@gmail.com.

Opinion and Commentary

The Westwood Times publishes opinion columns, analysis pieces, and editorials in addition to straight news reporting. These pieces are clearly labeled as opinion or commentary and are distinct from our news reporting. Opinion content represents the views of the author or, in the case of unsigned editorials, the editorial board. Readers should not interpret opinion content as established fact.

Content Disclaimer

While The Westwood Times takes all reasonable steps to ensure the accuracy and completeness of its reporting, published content is provided for informational purposes only. News reporting reflects information available at the time of publication and may be subject to change as events develop. The Westwood Times does not warrant the accuracy, completeness, or fitness for a particular purpose of any content on this site.

Nothing on this site constitutes legal, financial, medical, or professional advice. Readers should consult qualified professionals for guidance on specific matters.

Third-Party Links and Sources
