Ryan's Weekly Mashup: Edition 1

The inaugural edition of the Westwood social calendar you didn't know you needed. Three events. Three days. Zero excuses.

By The Westwood Times Staff
3 min read
Updated April 5, 2026

Welcome to the first edition of the Mashup. Every week, this column breaks down what is actually worth your time on the social calendar so you are not standing around a dorm hallway at 10 p.m. wondering what happened to your Thursday. Lock it in.

Thursday: Jameson's Pregame at the Classic

We are kicking off the first week the right way. The Thursday pregame is Jameson's-anchored, and yes, we are bringing back the BORG. If you do not know what a BORG is by now, you have been spending too much time in Powell. It is the first week of the quarter, it is being held at one of the Classic Halls, and the energy will match the setting. This is the one to show up to early. Doors are not formal, the vibe is. Lock it in.

Friday: ZBT x Soulja Boy

Zeta Beta Tau is hosting Friday, and the theme does not need much explanation: Soulja Boy. If you are not already mentally prepared to crank that, Superman somebody, and have a legitimately good Friday night, then this column cannot help you. ZBT throws a clean event and this one has the theme to match the energy. Clear your Saturday morning obligations now. We are flying high.

Sunday: Exclusive Amazon Prime Video Screening at Mark 703

Sunday is the wildcard and it is the best one on paper. There is an exclusive advance screening of Balls Up happening at Mark 703, the new Mark Wahlberg comedy from Peter Farrelly that drops on Amazon Prime Video April 15. The event is sponsored by Prime Video. Merch may also make an appearance, details pending. Invites are limited and most have already gone out.

If you have already been contacted, you know the details. If you have not, the window is closing. Sunday afternoon. Mark 703.

That is Edition 1. More to come next week. Stay locked in, Bruins.
